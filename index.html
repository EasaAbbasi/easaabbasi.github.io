About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics and a member of the Sensorimotor Interaction Group. I received my Ph.D. degree from Koc University in January 2024. During my Ph.D. studies, I worked on characterizing electroadhesive tactile displays and understanding the human sense of touch. My current research interests include haptics, human-computer interaction (HCI), and the intersection of haptics and AI. In my research, I aim to develop methods and tools for understanding human sense of touch, developing AI models to enhance haptics, and developing tactile displays to convey sensory information.
