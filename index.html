About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics and a member of the Sensorimotor Interaction Group. My research focuses on haptics, HCI, and the intersection of haptics and AI.

Research Interests

  • Haptics
  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
  • Haptics and Artificial Intelligence

Projects

AI for Haptics

Scene2Hap
